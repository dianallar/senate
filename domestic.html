Domestic Policy

Building a Pacifica that works for all; From the ground, up.

“Empowered women empower nations.”

For decades, women have fought for the right to control our own bodies, but today, those freedoms are under siege. As a mother, a survivor, and someone who has had an abortion before, I know firsthand how deeply personal these decisions are. No politician should ever dictate what Pacifican women do with their bodies.

As your Senator, I will:

  • Codify Roe v. Wade's protections into the constitution, guaranteeing abortion access nationwide.
  • Eliminate taxes on menstrual products, contraception, and prenatal care, because basic healthcare and the means to make your own decisions are not luxuries, or, at the very least, they should not be, in the greatest nation on the face of the planet.
  • Expand the Violence Against Women Act to include LGBTQ+ survivors and fund rape crisis centers in every state.

This fight does not stop at a woman's right to choose and make her own medical decisions. It's also about justice:

Pay equity. Women still earn 82 cents for every dollar men make—and it's worse for Black, Latina, and Native women. We'll enforce equal pay laws and fine corporations that discriminate.

Parental support. Universal childcare. Paid family leave. Protections for pregnant workers. No one should have to choose between their job and their family.

Bodily sovereignty. Ban forced sterilizations of disabled and incarcerated women. End the tampon tax. Prosecute domestic abusers to the fullest extent of the law—no exceptions.

Republicans want to drag us back to the 1950s. We won't let them. Together, we'll build a future where every woman, cis or trans, has the freedom to thrive.

“Education is the passport to the future.”

Every child in America deserves access to a world-class public education - regardless of their zip code, family income, or background. As a former school counselor and the mother of a public school student, I've seen firsthand how our current system fails too many children. We must invest in our schools, respect our teachers, and prepare students for the future. As Lieutenant Governor of Olympia, I introduced and passed the PUTA Act, which substantially increased the minimum wage for teachers. I'd like to implement a similar policy on the national stage.

As your Senator, I will:

  • End the teacher shortage crisis with a 15% federal wage subsidy and student loan forgiveness for educators
  • Fix our broken school funding model by replacing property taxes with federal needs-based funding
  • Guarantee mental health support with at least two counselors per school and universal mental health support cost coverage
  • Make college and trade school debt-free through federal subsidies and income-contingent repayment plans
  • Modernize our curriculum with comprehensive sex education, civics education, renewed geography and geopolitical courses, vocational training, and climate literacy

Key Priorities:

Early Childhood: Universal pre-K for all 3- and 4-year-olds, with living wages for childcare workers

K-12 Reform: Smaller class sizes (25:1 max ratio), updated facilities, and free school meals

Higher Education: Tuition-free community college and expanded Pell Grants

Vocational Training: Federal partnerships with unions to create apprenticeship pipelines

For too long, we've underfunded our schools while overtesting our children. My administration will actually invest in the next generation - because education isn't an expense, it's the foundation of our democracy and our economy.

“Dignity in work, pride in progress.”

The American labor movement built the middle class - but for decades, corporate interests and Republican politicians have waged war on workers' rights. As the author of the Collective Bargaining Rights Amendment, I'm fighting to enshrine union rights in our Constitution because no worker should have to beg for fair pay and safe conditions.

My Constitutional Amendment Will:

  • Permanently guarantee the right to unionize and collectively bargain
  • Ban "right-to-work" laws that weaken unions
  • Protect striking workers from retaliation
  • Establish sectoral bargaining so unions can negotiate industry-wide standards

As President, I signed into law the Right to Disconnect Act, which ensures that workers can disengage from work communications during non-work hours without fear of repercussions. I also signed into law the Employment Mobility and Competition Act, which banned non-compete agreements and non-disclosure agreements focused on assault, and the Industry Finance and Insurance Act, which revitalized domestic investment in manufacturing and provided a support boost for United States Exporters, protecting jobs and strengthening our economy.

As a Senator, these will be my priorities in regards to Labor and the Workforce:

Modern Worker Protections:
32-hour workweek • Paid family leave • Mandatory breaks • Predictable scheduling

Rebuild Union Power:
Card check recognition • First contract arbitration • Ban permanent replacements

Living Wages:
$25 federal minimum wage for teachers • Equal pay certification • Tip credit elimination

From the picket lines to the factory floors, American workers deserve dignity. My platform will finally give American labor the protections it deserves - because when our economy is strong, America is strong.

“America’s strength is found in its diversity.”

America is a nation of immigrants - but our broken system traps millions in uncertainty while failing to secure our borders. My plan combines compassion with common sense: expanding legal pathways while cracking down on smuggling cartels and employers who exploit undocumented workers. Pacifica is a very diverse state, rich in culture spanning hundreds of years, several communities, nations, and populations. As your Senator, I will work to ensure that all who wish to come to America (hopefully Pacifica!) and call this place their home can do so freely, for everyone should be allowed to partake in the project that is the greatest nation in the world. I plan to prioritize several policies that will make immigration a more humane process, including:

Pathway to Citizenship:

  • Immediate status for Dreamers, farmworkers, and essential workers
  • 8-year citizenship path for undocumented immigrants with clean records
  • Vocational fast-track: Earn citizenship through skilled work in high-need areas

Border Security & Reform:

  • Triple asylum officers to clear backlog while maintaining due process
  • Smart surveillance with drones and sensors instead of wasteful walls
  • Anti-cartel task force with DEA and international partners

Worker Protections:

  • Ban ICE workplace raids that punish workers instead of exploitative employers
  • Expand H-2B visas with union wage requirements
  • Whistleblower visas for workers who report labor violations

We'll replace chaos with order - welcoming those who follow the rules while holding accountable those who break them. Our plan strengthens America's workforce while finally fixing this unsustainable system.

“There is no Planet B.”

Climate change isn't a distant threat - it's burning through our neighborhoods and flooding our streets today. My plan meets this crisis with the urgency it demands while creating millions of union jobs in clean energy. We'll lead the world in innovation without leaving workers behind. Throughout my careeer, I have supported every bill introduced by my colleagues Ducky Anka and Threes Twos to mitigate our negative environmental impact and move the United States towards a greener future that ensures the long-term stability and longevity of our planet and our environment. As your Senator, I will take several more crucial steps past those perpetuated by Senator Anka and Former President Twos to ensure that we continue the American vision of giving more back to the world than we take and fulfilling the promise of a new tomorrow for all. Here are some of those steps that I plan to take:

Climate Action Now:

  • 100% clean electricity by 2035 through renewable investments and grid modernization
  • Civilian Climate Corps employing 1.5 million Americans to restore ecosystems
  • Carbon tariff on polluting imports to protect U.S. industry

Environmental Justice:

  • Replace every lead pipe in America within 5 years
  • Ban new fossil fuel projects near communities of color
  • Superfund cleanup acceleration with polluter pay requirements

Green Industrial Revolution:

  • Union-made requirement for all federal clean energy projects
  • American Battery Initiative to dominate EV supply chains
  • Farmers Conservation Corps paying landowners for carbon sequestration

This is about creating good-paying jobs and protecting our planet while saving our communities. We'll beat the climate crisis and build the most competitive economy in the world.

“Building bridges to a better tomorrow.”

America's infrastructure is crumbling while other nations speed ahead. My plan will create millions of union jobs rebuilding our country with smart investments in sustainable transit, resilient systems, and connected communities. In recent years, since 2019 more specifically, the United States has made numerous advancements in infrastructure, much to the benefit of our communities, but there is always room for improvement. Creating connectivity is a hefty project. I was proud to cosponsor Ducky Anka's Transportation Equity and Tolling Reform Act (TETRA) in the Senate, which made it illegal to use highway tolls for anything other than infrastructure improvement, maintenance, and repair. As your Senator, I implement several initiatives to continue to increase our national infrastructure standard, including:

Modernizing America's Backbone:

  • National high-speed regional connector rail network connecting all major metros by 2040, in consultation with President Fields
  • Complete Streets Initiative redesigning 100,000 miles of roads for bikes, buses and pedestrians
  • Electric Vehicle Revolution with 500,000 new charging stations and domestic manufacturing

Community-Centered Projects:

  • Neighborhood Infrastructure Grants letting communities prioritize local needs
  • Universal Broadband through municipal fiber and 5G cooperatives
  • Clean Water for All replacing every lead service line in America

Workforce & Innovation:

  • Project Labor Agreements required for all federal infrastructure projects
  • Infrastructure Academy training workers for green construction jobs
  • ARPA-Infrastructure funding next-gen materials and smart city tech

In the Senate, I will work to reimagine how America moves, starting with fixing potholes. From rural broadband to bullet trains, we'll build infrastructure worthy of our people and our future.

“Justice for all, not just for some.”

Our justice system should keep communities safe while upholding human dignity. For too long, we’ve confused punishment with justice and incarceration with safety. My plan reforms this broken system—from policing to prisons to reentry—focusing on prevention, fairness, and second chances. I have long advocated for a fairer justice system that achieves its ultimate purpose: Enforcing the law in a reasonable, fair, and just manner. As your Senator, I will work to realize that vision through the following policies:

Police Accountability & Reform:

  • National use-of-force standard banning chokeholds and no-knock warrants
  • Demilitarize police by ending 1033 program transfers of military gear
  • Community Safety Grants to fund mental health responders instead of armed officers for crisis calls
  • Increasing the minimum police training period to ensure that our officers are properly trained and capable of carrying out their jobs

Prison & Sentencing Reform:

  • End mandatory minimums for nonviolent offenses
  • Ban private prisons and ICE detention centers
  • Elder parole for aging inmates who pose no public threat

Rehabilitation & Reentry:

  • Pell Grant access for incarcerated students
  • Ban the Box for housing and employment nationwide
  • Reentry passports providing ID, job training, and housing assistance

Victims' Rights & Prevention:

  • Rape kit backlog elimination with federal funding and tracking
  • Restorative justice programs as alternatives to incarceration
  • Youth diversion programs to break the school-to-prison pipeline

True justice isn’t about cages—it’s about accountability, healing, and a path forward. We’ll make our system fair, effective, and worthy of a nation founded on second chances.

“Home is where opportunity begins.”

Safe, stable housing is the foundation of strong communities - yet, skyrocketing rents and corporate landlords have pushed millions to the brink. My plan guarantees housing as a human right through bold federal action. Throughout my career, I have consistently stood beside plans to bolster our national infrastructure, and that includes housing. As your Senator, here are some things I will strive for to end the housing crisis and lower housing costs for everyone:

Immediate Relief:

  • National rent stabilization capping annual increases at 3% or inflation (whichever is lower)
  • Emergency tenant protections banning no-cause evictions and unfair fees
  • Direct rental assistance for households spending over 30% of income on housing

Build Generational Wealth:

  • First-generation homebuyer grants covering 20% down payments
  • Community land trusts preserving affordability in gentrifying neighborhoods
  • Triple funding for Section 8 vouchers until waitlists are eliminated

Crush Speculation:

  • 25% tax on corporate home purchases
  • Vacancy penalties for landlords hoarding empty units
  • Public housing construction adding 5 million units by 2035

End Homelessness:

  • Housing First guaranteeing shelter with wraparound services
  • Repurpose vacant offices as supportive housing
  • Mental health/addiction treatment integrated into all shelters

"Your zip code shouldn't determine your destiny. We'll make housing affordable for all, not a casino for Wall Street and corrupt landlords."
