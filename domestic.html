Domestic Policy

Building a Pacifica that works for all; From the ground, up.

“Empowered women empower nations.”

For decades, women have fought for the right to control our own bodies, but today, those freedoms are under siege. As a mother, a survivor, and someone who has had an abortion before, I know firsthand how deeply personal these decisions are. No politician should ever dictate what Pacifican women do with their bodies.

As your Senator, I will:

  • Codify Roe v. Wade's protections into the constitution, guaranteeing abortion access nationwide.
  • Eliminate taxes on menstrual products, contraception, and prenatal care, because basic healthcare and the means to make your own decisions are not luxuries, or, at the very least, they should not be, in the greatest nation on the face of the planet.
  • Expand the Violence Against Women Act to include LGBTQ+ survivors and fund rape crisis centers in every state.

This fight does not stop at a woman's right to choose and make her own medical decisions. It's also about justice:

Pay equity. Women still earn 82 cents for every dollar men make—and it's worse for Black, Latina, and Native women. We'll enforce equal pay laws and fine corporations that discriminate.

Parental support. Universal childcare. Paid family leave. Protections for pregnant workers. No one should have to choose between their job and their family.

Bodily sovereignty. Ban forced sterilizations of disabled and incarcerated women. End the tampon tax. Prosecute domestic abusers to the fullest extent of the law—no exceptions.

Republicans want to drag us back to the 1950s. We won't let them. Together, we'll build a future where every woman, cis or trans, has the freedom to thrive.
