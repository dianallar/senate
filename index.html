Meet Diana

Former President Diana Allar is committed to serving the people of Pacifica with integrity and dedication. Representing Utah's 3rd District, she has made many meaningful advancements in the United States Congress to benefit the people of Pacifica and America at large.

Serving in the United States Senate, as Speaker of the United States House of Representatives, Governor and Lieutenant Governor of Olympia, House Minority Leader, DNC Chair, DCCC Chair, Party Leader of the Democrats, Secretary of the Interior, as President of the United States, and now as Chief of Staff and White House Press Secretary, Diana Allar's public service knows no bounds.

Diana Allar introduced the Fix The Public Option Act to the Senate, which brought health insurance to eight million uninsured Americans. She introduced UECDEA into the House, which, upon passage, will make preschool education universally accessible for all parents and children in the United States.

Key Issues

Lowering Inflation

Creating disinflationary measures to lower costs for Americans and get our economy back on track. For too long has the government allowed inflation to increase with no remedy.

Universal Preschool

Ensuring every Pacifican has access to preschool education for their child, rural and urban alike, and that the education and care provided is excellent in quality.

Collective Bargaining Rights

Allowing our Union laborers to organize and negotiate for better wages and working conditions, and enshrining those rights into our nation's Constitution so that they may not be trampled.
